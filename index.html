Welcome

The Isle of Wight Web Mapping Project is designed to empower the local council with cutting-edge geospatial data and interactive maps. By integrating data on soil characteristics, road networks, population, rainfall, flood risk, and tourism, this platform provides crucial insights to support strategic planning and decision-making.

Our web application leverages a variety of mapping technologies—from the ArcGIS API for JavaScript to ArcGIS Online embeds—to offer a comprehensive view of the island’s spatial data. Whether you are assessing environmental impacts, planning infrastructure, or enhancing tourist experiences, our platform delivers detailed, actionable information.

  • Soil Characteristics: Detailed analysis for sustainable land use and agriculture.
  • Road Networks: Comprehensive mapping of transport routes for planning and emergency management.
  • Towns & Population: Insightful demographic data for urban planning and community development.
  • Average Rainfall: Precipitation data critical for environmental management and water resource planning.
  • Flood Risk: Spatial analysis of flood-prone areas to help mitigate hazards.
  • Tourism Highlights: Showcasing the island’s attractions to boost local tourism.
