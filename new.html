Welcome

Welcome to the Isle of Wight Web Mapping Project. Our website integrates a wide range of mapping techniques using state-of-the-art tools. Explore our fixed themes--Soil Characteristics, Roads, Towns with Population, Island Outline, and Average Rainfall--along with extra layers such as Demographics and Flood Risk.

Designed for the Isle of Wight Council, our platform provides clear, visual context to complex spatial data, empowering smarter decision-making.
